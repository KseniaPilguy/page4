For such apps it is vital to plan modernization in advance and be ready to adjust and distribute processes it works for minimal disruption.

Low quality+High value

This software should be modernized primarily. It is crucial for business and at the same time its technological state leaves much to be desired, that fraught with failures. Downtimes are dangerous in case with the application of high business value, as we can’t predict the level of damage.

Low quality+Low value

Most likely this software should be replaced with a package provided by specialized companies, because it’s not expedient to modernize such system considering low value and bad tech state. Packages can easily process low-priority tasks being affordable and not requiring any maintenance efforts.

High quality+Low value

Such application works great and doesn't need any tech improvements, so that it can be used till tech value becomes low. As it is not a significant assess for a business its modernization or replacement in the future will barely affect business processes.
